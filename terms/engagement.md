The term **engagement** refers to the process of working collaboratively with and through groups of people affiliated with a project by geographic proximity, special interest, or similar situations—often involving partnerships that help to influence system- and process-design, to define relationships among partners, and to serve as catalysts for changing policies, programs, and practices.
